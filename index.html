Our Story

Where Nature
Meets the Lake

Nestled along the serene shores of Murchison Bay on Lake Victoria, Jahazi Pier is Kampala's most beloved lakeside sanctuary — a place where the city's pace dissolves into the gentle rhythm of lapping water.

From the moment you arrive, you are enveloped by manicured lawns, towering fruit trees, and the calming presence of the lake. Wildlife moves freely here — marabou storks wade at the shoreline, colourful kingfishers dart between branches, and playful vervet monkeys keep watch from above.

Our chefs craft every dish with fresh-caught fish from the lake, local produce, and a passion for authentic East African flavours. Whether you come for a quiet afternoon with a book, a family picnic, a celebratory event, or a boat ride into the sunset — Jahazi Pier is the lakeside escape you have always needed.
